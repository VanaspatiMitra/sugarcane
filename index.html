About the Crop:

Sugarcane - Co 0238 is a high-yielding variety known for its excellent sugar content and adaptability. It thrives in well-drained, loamy to clay loam soils with a pH of 6.0 to 7.5. The crop requires approximately 1,200-1,500 mm of water per acre, provided through rainfall or irrigation. Typically, Co 0238 yields around 80-100 tons of cane per acre. Profit margins are generally strong, ranging from 15-25%, depending on input costs and market prices.

  • Soil Composition: Well-drained, loamy to sandy loam soil. pH range of 6.0 to 7.5
  • Water need per Acre: 3.94 to 4.92feet
  • Potential Dangers: Chemical use, Soil Degradation, Pest Infestation, Waterlogging
  • Fertilizers: Diammonium Phosphate, Organic Matter, Potassium, Nitrogen
  • Pesticides: Carbofuran , Imidacloprid ,Dimethoate, Mancozeb.
